Accommodation Information

We can’t wait to celebrate with you at The Millhouse! To make your trip as seamless as possible, we’ve compiled information about staying onsite and nearby. Whether you’re interested in the convenience of staying with us at The Millhouse or exploring nearby options, we’ve got you covered.
Staying at the Millhouse?

We are thrilled to offer limited onsite accommodations at The Millhouse for our guests. Staying here means you’ll be right at the heart of the celebration, with no need to worry about transportation to and from the venue.
  • Cost: On average rooms at The Millhouse range from approximately €95 to €135 per night per person for adults. There are exceptions and lower cost options on site as well.
Booking Process:
  • When you RSVP, you’ll have the option to indicate if you’re interested in staying onsite.
  • Once RSVPs close, we’ll confirm availability and let you know whether we can accommodate your request.
What if we're full?
  • If we’re unable to offer you a room onsite, don’t worry! There are many wonderful alternate accommodations options for you to stay near (some are listed below) and we will work with you to make sure you are setup with options.
Travel Tip: If you’re interested in staying onsite but want peace of mind about having a backup, we recommend booking an alternative option with free cancellation.
